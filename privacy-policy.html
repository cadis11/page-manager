Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Rudrakshya Pasal ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our Instagram Page Manager application ("the App"). The App is a business tool used solely to manage our own Instagram business account communications.

By using this App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Access

The App accesses the following data through the Meta/Instagram Graph API:

  • Instagram Business Account profile information (name, account ID)
  • Direct messages (DMs) sent to our Instagram business account
  • Comments on our Instagram business posts
  • Basic page engagement data

This data is accessed solely for the purpose of managing our business communications on Instagram.

How We Use the Information

Information accessed through this App is used exclusively to:

  • Read and respond to customer messages on our Instagram account
  • Manage and moderate comments on our posts
  • Provide customer support to our followers and customers
  • Operate and improve our internal business communication workflows

Data Storage & Security

The App operates as a client-side tool and does not store any Instagram messages, user data, or personal information on external servers or databases.

  • No messages or user data are saved to any third-party server
  • Access tokens are stored locally in your browser session only
  • All API calls are made directly to Meta's official Graph API
  • Data is not retained after your session ends

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any data accessed through this App with third parties. The App is for internal business use only and all information remains strictly within our organization.

Third-Party Services

This App uses the Meta/Instagram Graph API. Your use of Instagram is governed by Meta's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, which can be found at facebook.com/privacy/policy.

Your Rights

If you have communicated with our Instagram business account and wish to request deletion or access to your data, please contact us directly. We will respond to all reasonable requests within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy
